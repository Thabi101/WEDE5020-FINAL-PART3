About GradLink Hub

We are more than just a job board. We are a bridge connecting the academic world to the professional landscape of South Africa.

Bridging the Gap

Founded with a passion for youth development, GradLink Hub was born out of a simple observation: South African graduates possess incredible talent, yet many face significant barriers when transitioning into their first professional roles.

We set out to create a centralized platform that doesn't just list jobs, but actively prepares students through career guidance, skill development, and direct mentorship from industry experts.

Verified Partners & Opportunities

GradLink Hub works with verified employers, training providers, and mentorship networks to help graduates move confidently into the workforce.

Students graduating and entering the workforce

We support graduates with career planning, internship placement, and skills coaching.

10K+ Graduates Connected

Thousands of learners have connected with jobs, mentorship, and career resources through our platform.

Our Core Values

Mission

Vision

Commitment
